Vicki Crosson for Secretary

Sr Engineer in DevP - Remote from Boston

I've been a union organizer since 2019, going back to the olden days when it was a secret! With experience as a shop steward, the current unit secretary, a member of the past bargaining committee, and an officer at our Local (NYGuild), I've seen all sides of building our power (and learned a lot along the way!) I'm running for unit secretary again to ensure that every member's voice is heard.

My priorities include:

  • Empowering members to get involved, in whatever way you can
  • Seeking your input, to drive our actions
  • Keeping resources organized and available to anyone who needs them
  • Making sure you find community in your union!

Outside of our unit, I'm active with my town's Disability Commission, served as NewsGuild representative to the CWA Accessibility Working group, and speak about the intersection of disability justice and labor whenever I can. I enjoy quilting, and I have 2 cats: Roger Roger & Maz (pictured).
